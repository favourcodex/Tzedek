About Tzedek

Tzedek is a revolutionary E-commerce platform that embodies the principles of justice, equality, and community. Our name, inspired by the Hebrew word for "justice" or "righteousness," reflects our commitment to creating a fair, seamless and inclusive online marketplace. We have incorporated a robust network plan for all stores owners, so as to enjoy extra passive income.

Our Mission

At Tzedek, we empower entrepreneurs and businesses to thrive in the digital age. Our platform provides a seamless and secure environment for individuals to rent virtual shops, showcase their products, and offer services to a global audience.

Our Vision

We envision a world where anyone can turn their passion into a successful online business, regardless of geographical location or financial resources. Our platform is designed to foster innovation, creativity, and fair competition, enabling businesses to grow and succeed in a rapidly evolving digital landscape

Our Values

  • Fairness: We strive to create a level playing field for all businesses, regardless of size or background.
  • Community: We foster connections and collaborations among our members, promoting mutual support and growth.
  • Innovation: We continuously update and improve our platform to meet the evolving needs of our users.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, services, and policies.

General

Tzedek is a community-driven global marketplace that empowers entrepreneurs to sell their products and services online through virtual shop rentals. Our platform provides the tools, resources, and support needed to grow your business globally.

Getting started is easy! Simply register for an account, choose your preferred shop package, set up your virtual shop, and start listing your products or services. Our step-by-step guide will help you through the entire process.

Tzedek stands out with our community-driven approach. We focus on empowering entrepreneurs through education, support, and networking opportunities. Our platform is designed to help businesses thrive globally while maintaining their unique identity and brand.

Shop Rentals

Virtual shop rentals provide you with a dedicated online space within our marketplace where you can showcase and sell your products or services. Each shop comes with various features depending on the package you choose.

Yes! All shop packages allow for customization to match your brand identity. Higher-tier packages offer more extensive customization options, including layout designs, color schemes, and advanced branding features.

Shop rentals are available on monthly or annual plans. Annual plans come with a significant discount. You can upgrade your package at any time as your business grows.

Selling & Services

You can sell physical products, digital goods, or services on our platform. However, all items must comply with our community guidelines and legal requirements. Some restricted items include illegal goods, dangerous substances, and counterfeit products.

You're responsible for shipping your physical products to customers. Our platform integrates with major shipping providers to help you generate shipping labels and track deliveries. You can set your shipping rates and methods in your shop settings.

Absolutely! Many entrepreneurs on our platform offer services like consulting, digital courses, tutoring, design work, and more. Our platform has special features designed for service-based businesses.

Payments & Fees

When customers make purchases, payments are processed through our secure payment system. Funds are then transferred to your connected bank account or payment method according to the payment schedule you've selected (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly).

Tzedek charges a monthly shop rental fee based on your selected package. Additionally, there's a small transaction fee (3-5% depending on your package) for each sale to cover payment processing costs. There are no hidden fees or charges.

Customers can pay using major credit cards, debit cards, mobile payment services, and local payment methods depending on their region. Premium and Enterprise packages support additional payment options like cryptocurrency and buy-now-pay-later services.
